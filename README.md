### 🚀 Key Focus Areas

1.  **Production MLOps:** Lifecycle management of ML models using **MLflow** for tracking, **DVC** for versioning, and **BentoML** for serving.
2.  **Autonomous Multi-Agent Systems:** Engineering collaborative AI swarms using **CrewAI**, **LangGraph**, and **Microsoft AutoGen**.
3.  **Advanced RAG Pipelines:** Building enterprise-grade retrieval systems featuring **Hybrid Search**, **Re-ranking**, and **Long-Context** processing.
4.  **Model Optimization:** Quantization of frontier models using **GGUF, AWQ, and QLoRA** for efficient local and edge deployment.
5.  **Generative AI Fine-Tuning:** Specializing in parameter-efficient fine-tuning (PEFT) for domain-specific LLM adaptation.
6.  **Computer Vision & Multimodality:** Deploying Vision Transformers (ViT) and CNNs for complex high-dimensional spatial reasoning.
7.  **Data Engineering Pipelines:** Designing scalable ETL and workflow orchestration using **Apache Airflow** and **Kubeflow**.
8.  **Cloud Native Architecture:** Deploying scalable AI backends on **AWS SageMaker** and **Azure AI Studio** utilizing Docker/Kubernetes.
9.  **Predictive Analytics at Scale:** Utilizing **XGBoost, CatBoost, and LightGBM** for high-performance tabular data forecasting.
10. **Interactive AI Interfaces:** Developing sophisticated human-in-the-loop (HITL) dashboards using **Streamlit**, **Gradio**, and **FastAPI**.
